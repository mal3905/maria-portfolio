Travel Quiz

Quiz App is an interactive application that allows a user to be prompted through a series of 5 multiple choice questions regarding their knowledge of world landmarks and important world events. Feedback is generated after every answer is submitted. If the wrong answered is submitted, user receives feedback with correct answer choice. This app keeps track of the user score as they move on to the next questions and the total score is generated at the end.
